How it works:

This application uses YOLOv11 (the latest version) to detect objects in real-time from your webcam. The model identifies common objects and displays bounding boxes around them with labels.

The Gemini AI features provide enhanced spatial reasoning and can analyze lunch tray contents to help sort items into the proper disposal categories (trash, recycling, compost, or dish return).

Features: Enhanced accuracy and speed with YOLOv11 technology, focused on detecting food and dining-related items, plus Gemini AI spatial reasoning.

Note: The first run might take a moment as the models are loaded.
